Meet The Monsters - An Interactive Book

Meet the Monsters is an interactive children’s book geared towards preschool aged children. Using our basic senses, especially the sense of touch, is important for early childhood development. This interactive book employs a variety of sensors and outputs to appeal to the senses of a young child. The concept behind this book was to create something which is alive and active.
